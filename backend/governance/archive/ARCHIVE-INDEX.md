# Governance Archive Index

This directory preserves historical governance documents for transparency and audit traceability.

The archived materials reflect prior governance states of the Yunivera system and are retained for record continuity.

## Regulatory Freeze (v1.0)

### Period
Initiated: 2025  
Concluded: 17.02.2026  

### Purpose of Freeze

The Regulatory Freeze (v1.0) was introduced as a precautionary governance measure during an early-stage architectural phase.

Its purpose was to:

- Temporarily suspend public-facing system exposure
- Reassess structural positioning and system scope
- Review infrastructure boundaries and deployment posture
- Clarify the long-term architectural direction of the project

The freeze was preventative in nature and did not result from regulatory enforcement or compliance violation.

No regulatory violations occurred.

---

### Operational Conditions During Freeze

During the freeze:

- No new production deployments were introduced
- Public system access remained suspended
- Internal development was restricted and evaluated
- Governance policies were drafted and formalized

The freeze functioned as a stabilization interval.

---

### Conclusion of Freeze

The Regulatory Freeze (v1.0) formally concluded on 17.02.2026.

It was replaced by:

**Strategic Pause (Governance v2.0)**

The transition reflects:

- Completion of structural reassessment
- Establishment of PR-based audit workflow
- Implementation of branch protections and governance controls
- Formalization of layered architectural discipline

The system transitioned from reactive freeze posture to proactive structural governance.

---

### Approval Authority

The transition from Regulatory Freeze (v1.0) to Strategic Pause (v2.0) was internally authorized under project governance authority.

No external regulatory directive was involved.

- 17.02.2026 — Governance v2.0 framework established
